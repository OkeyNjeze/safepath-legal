1 Introduction

Welcome to Live Intervals, a product of Njeze Technologies. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it when you use our mobile application.

By downloading or using Live Intervals, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, we kindly ask that you do not use the app.

2 Who We Are

Live Intervals is a family safety and location tracking application built to help families stay connected and protect one another. The app is developed and operated by Njeze Technologies, an independent software development company based in Nigeria. We are committed to full compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and all other applicable data protection regulations.

3 Minimum Age

Live Intervals is designed for users who are 13 years of age or older. Any user between the ages of 13 and 17 is required to obtain the consent of a parent or legal guardian before using the app.

By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 13 years old and, if under 18, that you have received the necessary parental or guardian approval.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Should we become aware that a user is below this age threshold, we will promptly delete their account and all associated data without notice.

Parents or guardians who suspect their child under 13 has registered an account are encouraged to contact us at liveintervals@gmail.com. We will investigate and respond within 7 days.

4 Information We Collect

4.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name — used to identify you within your family group
  • Email address — used for account creation, login and communication
  • Secret disguise code — stored entirely on your device and never transmitted to our servers

4.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • GPS location data — captured at intervals you personally select, only while tracking is actively enabled
  • Device information — device model, operating system version, app version and timezone, collected by Firebase solely to support app functionality

4.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Payment card details — all transactions are processed by Paystack. We only receive a transaction reference to confirm payment
  • Contacts, photos or camera data — we do not access anything outside the scope of this app
  • Advertising or analytics data — we currently do not engage in third party tracking. If this changes, this policy will be updated

5 How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively to deliver the services Live Intervals was built for:

  • Provide real-time location tracking and family safety features
  • Make your location history available to family members you have approved
  • Facilitate family invitations and group management
  • Process your premium subscription payments through Paystack
  • Maintain, improve and troubleshoot the app
We do not use your personal data for advertising, behavioural profiling or any purpose not listed above.

6 Location Data

Location data is at the heart of what Live Intervals does, and we handle it with great care:

  • Location is only captured when you consciously enable tracking within the app
  • Your location is visible exclusively to family members you have personally invited and accepted
  • Location history is automatically deleted after 24 hours for free plan users
  • Premium subscribers retain up to 30 days of location history before automatic deletion
  • You may stop tracking at any time directly from the app
  • No individual or organisation outside your approved family group has access to your location

7 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data under any circumstances. There are limited situations in which your data may be shared, all of which are necessary for the app to function:

  • Your family group — location data is shared only with members you have explicitly invited and accepted
  • Google Firebase — used to store and manage app data securely
  • Paystack — processes subscription payments and manages all related financial data independently
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose information if compelled by Nigerian law or a valid legal order

8 Data Retention

  • Free plan users — location history automatically deleted every 24 hours
  • Premium subscribers — location history retained for up to 30 days before automatic deletion
  • Account data — retained for as long as your account remains active
  • After account deletion — data removed from our systems immediately. Residual backup copies may take up to 7 days to be fully purged

9 Data Security

Protecting your data is a responsibility we take seriously. All data is stored on Google Firebase, which enforces strict access control rules ensuring that no user can view another user's data without explicit permission. Payment information is managed entirely by Paystack and never passes through our systems. Your disguise secret code exists only on your personal device and is inaccessible to us.

We must be honest: no digital system is completely immune to risk. While we have implemented strong safeguards, we cannot provide an absolute guarantee of security.

10 Your Rights Under the NDPA 2023

As a user in Nigeria, the Nigeria Data Protection Act grants you the following rights:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction — request corrections to any inaccurate information
  • Right to deletion — delete your account within the app or contact us at liveintervals@gmail.com and we will action your request within 7 days
  • Right to withdraw consent — stop location tracking at any time by disabling it within the app

11 The Disguise Feature

Live Intervals offers an optional disguise feature that allows the app to appear as a different, ordinary application on your device. This feature exists purely for personal safety purposes. The secret code that activates this feature is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to our servers.

We strongly advise users to remember their disguise code. We have no mechanism to retrieve or reset it on your behalf.

12 Third Party Services

  • Google Firebase — data storage and authentication. Privacy policy at firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Maps — in-app mapping features. Privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy
  • Paystack — subscription payment processing. Privacy policy at paystack.com/privacy
